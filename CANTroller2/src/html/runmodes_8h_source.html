209// Here are the different runmodes documented
210//
211// ** Basic Mode **
212// - Required: BasicMode switch On
213// At startup it attempts to park the gas servo and brake motor, to release all tension on gas or brake
214// pedals so you can drive w/ your feet. Thereafter the gas and brake don't do anything. Only the steering
215// works, so use the pedals. This mode is enabled by a switch on the controller box. The only way to
216// leave Basic Mode is by turning off the basic switch, or turning off the syspower signal.
217//
218// ** Asleep Mode **
219// - Required: Request with hotrc ch4 button when shut down
220// Turns off power to the system. This includes all sensors/actuators and the screen, but not the hotrc receiver.
221// When requested to power up (same ch4 hotrc button), it re-powers everything and goes to shutdown mode.
222//
223// ** Shutdown Mode **
224// - Required: BasicMode switch Off & Ignition Off
225// This mode is active at boot, or whenever the ignition is off or when panic stopping. If the car is
226// moving, then like hold mode, shutdown mode will try to stop the car. Once stopped, then like basic mode,
227// it will park the motors out of the way and all systems stop. After a timeout it will go to asleep mode.
228//
229// ** Stall Mode **
230// - Required: Engine stopped & BasicMode switch Off & Ignition On
231// This mode is active when the engine is not running. If car is moving, then it presumably may
232// coast to a stop. The actuators are all enabled and work, but the gas drops to open-loop control.
233// The starter may be turned on or off freely here by hitting the hotrc ch4 button. If the engine
234// turns, then it'll go to hold mode. May be useful if beoing pushed or towed, or when servicing.
235//
236// ** Hold Mode **
237// - Required: Engine running & JoyVert<=Center & BasicMode switch Off & Ignition On
238// This mode ensures the car is stopped and stays stopped until you pull the trigger to give it gas, at which
239// point it goes to fly mode. This mode is entered from fly mode if the car comes to a stop, or from Stall Mode if
240// the engine starts turning. The starter can possibly be on through that transition, and thereafter it may be
241// turned off but not on from hold mode.
242//
243// ** Fly Mode **
244// - Required: JoyVert>Center & Engine running & BasicMode Off & Ign On
245// This mode is for driving under manual control. This mode is entered from hold mode by pulling the gas trigger.
246// If the trigger is released again before the car moves, it's back to hold mode though. Trigger pull controls throttle
247// and trigger push controls brake, either/or. Whenever the car stops, then back to hold mode. Cruise mode may be
248// entered from fly mode by pressing the cruise toggle button (ch4).
249//
250// ** Cruise Mode **
251// - Required: Car Moving & Engine running & BasicMode switch Off & Ignition On
252// This mode is entered from Fly Mode by pushing the cruise toggle button, and pushing it again will take you back.
253// There are no brakes in cruise mode, and pushing away on the trigger instead allows decreasing the cruise setpoint
254// (or pull it to increase the setpoint) Cruise can be run in three modes which adjust differently. The default
255// THROTTLE_DELTA mode holds a fixed throttle servo position as long as the trigger is centered, and if not,
256// it adjusts the setpoint up or down proportional to how far and how long you hold the trigger away from center.
257// If you panic and push full brake for over 500ms, it will drop to fly mode and then push brakes.
258//
259// ** Cal Mode **
260// This mode allows direct control of some actuators without respecting limits of motion, for purpose of
261// calibrating those very limits. It can be entered from shutdown mode with simulator on by long-pressing the CAL
262// button. Be careful with it.
